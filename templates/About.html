About

Welcome to our VEE tool website!


We are a passionate team of four students from Cairo University, united by our shared interest in leveraging advanced technologies to improve healthcare outcomes. Our journey began with a mission to contribute meaningfully to the field of reproductive medicine, specifically the in vitro fertilization (IVF) process.
As part of our graduation project, we developed an innovative tool that utilizes deep learning and transfer learning models to classify Sperm and Embryo as "good" or "bad", thereby aiding the IVF process.
Our tool, which we call VEE, stands as a testament to our dedication and hard work.

Our Mission

Our primary goal is to enhance the efficiency and success rates of IVF procedures
by providing an accurate, fast, reliable, and user-friendly tool for medical professionals.
By integrating advanced deep learning techniques into reproductive medicine,
we aim to offer hope and support to couples and individuals seeking to start or expand their families.

What We Offer

Sperm Classification Model:
Achieving an impressive test accuracy of 99%, this model ensures precise identification of healthy sperm,
which is crucial for the success of IVF treatments.

Embryo Classification Model:
With a test accuracy rate of 93%, our model aids in the selection of the best embryo
by identifying the healthy blastocyst embryo, increasing the chances of successful implantation and pregnancy.
